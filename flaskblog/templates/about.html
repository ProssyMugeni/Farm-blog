About us

Seeds of Gold.com was founded out of passion to bring together farmers from all walks of life to discuss issues and also share knowledge about farming.

The founder mrs Mugeni Prossy has over 2years of expertise in software development and is also a commercial farmer. With the challenges she faces as afarmer, she ralised that there was need for farmers to have a platform where they can meet and discuss solutions to the challenges and also share knoledge about afarming.
